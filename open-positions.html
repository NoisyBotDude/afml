Open Positions

We are constantly looking for highly qualified and motivated students to join our group. Students with outstanding academic career having education in Physics, Nanoscience,Chemical Science, Material Science or related areas of Engineering and Technology can apply for the following positions.


1. Post-Doctoral Position (minimum qualification required: Ph. D. thesis submitted/awarded with National Fellowship.)

2. Post-Doctoral Position (minimum qualification required: Ph. D. thesis awarded with UGC DS Kothari Fellowship.)

3. Post-Doctoral Fellows (RA) (minimum qualification required: Ph. D.)

4. Research Students (minimum qualification required: M. Sc./M. Tech, Only full-time doctoral students will be inducted)

5. Project Assistants (minimum qualification required: M. Sc./M.Tech)

They can also directly send their applications to Prof. P. Deb (pdeb@tezu.ernet.in) for future positions.
